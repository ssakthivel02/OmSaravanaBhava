Panchangam Data Framework

This module defines the data and editorial controls required before location-aware Murugan calendar guidance can be published.

Current boundary

No live Panchangam calculation is active. No tithi, nakshatra, sunrise, rahu kalam or festival time shown by this framework should be treated as a computed daily result.

Required inputs

  • Date and IANA timezone
  • Latitude, longitude and elevation
  • Authoritative ephemeris and ayanamsa policy
  • Temple or regional observance authority

Planned fields

  • Sunrise and sunset
  • Tithi and nakshatra intervals
  • Yoga and karana
  • Rahu kalam, yamagandam and gulika
  • Murugan observance qualification

Publication controls

  • Source identifier and calculation-engine version
  • Location and timezone displayed with every result
  • Uncertainty and regional variation notice
  • Human review for festival assertions

Machine-readable contract

The current non-computational status and planned schema are published in data/panchangam-framework.json.
