Arupadai Veedu

ஆறு படைவீடுகள்

The six sacred abodes of Lord Murugan across Tamil Nadu.

01

Tirupparamkunram

The mount of marriage and victory.

02

Tiruchendur

The seashore temple of Surasamharam.

03

Palani

The hill of wisdom and renunciation.

04

Swamimalai

The Guru abode where Om was taught.

05

Tiruttani

The hill of peace and divine rest.

06

Pazhamudircholai

The fertile forest shrine near Madurai.

Tiruchendur

Unlike the other five abodes, Tiruchendur rests beside the sea. It is revered as the place where Lord Murugan defeated Surapadman with the divine Vel.
