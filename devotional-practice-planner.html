Interpretation and privacy boundary

  • Plan storage contains only identifiers, names, descriptions, weekday numbers, same-origin route references, bounded check-ins and timestamps.
  • A local check-in records a date and route reference; it does not prove that the route was completely read, recited or spiritually completed.
  • Weekdays do not create notifications, calendar events, alarms or background monitoring.
  • Devotional text, selected passages, page bodies, images and audio files are not copied into plan storage.
  • Practice plans are registered as the seventh bounded dataset in the Personal Data Centre.
  • No streaks, scores, spiritual-outcome claims, analytics, recommendations, cloud synchronization or accounts are used.
