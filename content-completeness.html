Controlled ingestion contract

Every new song follows the same publication sequence.

Source evidence → canonical Tamil → easy-reading Tamil → transliteration → meaning → audio rights → human review → route, search, sitemap and PWA integration.

The machine-readable contract is stored at /schemas/murugan-song-record.schema.json. Records that fail source, rights or human-review checks remain in draft, source-required or withheld-rights-review status.
