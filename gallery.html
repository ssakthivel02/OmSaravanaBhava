Truthful visual publishing

Beautiful imagery must still be accurately described.

Temple illustrations, devotional artwork and decorative interface images are valuable parts of the experience, but they are not evidence of a temple’s current appearance. Future real photographs require photographer, source, licence and attribution metadata.

Devotional illustrationArtistic representation for devotional experience.

Documentary photographyReal-world photograph with source and usage rights. None is claimed unless explicitly labelled.

Future photography queue

Real temple photography requires a separate evidence workflow.

For each future photograph collect: temple/place identity, photographer or official source, original URL/file, capture or publication date where known, licence or written permission, required attribution, allowed transformations and a human confirmation that the image matches the stated location.
