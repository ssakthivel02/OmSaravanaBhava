Sources & Publication Policy

The publication framework separates source-linked publication from directory entries. A page is indexed only when its published claims are tied to an identified source or an explicitly labelled traditional association.

Claims deliberately deferred

The platform does not claim inscription dates, architectural chronology, daily temple timings, annual festival dates or a complete critical edition of any long devotional work unless a suitable authoritative source is available.
