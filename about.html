About Om Saravana Bhava

An independent Tamil-first devotional and educational platform dedicated to Lord Murugan—connecting sacred songs, temples, literature, festivals, audio, learning and transparent source evidence.

Explore the platformSource policyDevelopment roadmap

Tamil first, bilingual by design

Tamil titles and devotional context lead the experience, with clear English support for learners and the global Tamil diaspora.

Source-aware devotion

Verified full texts, reviewed extracts, source registers and rights-review records are separated so the platform does not claim completeness that has not been established.

Privacy-preserving technology

Static GitHub Pages, browser-local search, favourites, notes and collections reduce unnecessary accounts, tracking and server-side data collection.

Mission

Make Murugan devotion readable, discoverable and trustworthy.

Preserve

Organise source-linked Tamil devotional works and temple knowledge in durable, searchable formats.

Explain

Add easy-reading Tamil, transliteration and meaning without silently altering canonical text or copying protected commentary.

Connect

Link songs, temples, festivals, saints, stories, pilgrimage routes and audio through governed knowledge pathways.

Include

Support keyboard navigation, reduced motion, readable contrast, mobile use, offline access and multilingual learners.

Verify

Record source, edition, completeness, image provenance and audio rights before publication.

Improve openly

Use route recovery, content completeness and capability audits to show what is finished and what still needs evidence.

Independence and affiliation boundary

This is not presented as an official temple or religious-authority website.

Temple details, festival guidance and devotional explanations are educational references. Official timings, current rituals, travel restrictions and calendar dates should be confirmed with the relevant temple or authoritative calendar source. Any future official partnership or endorsement must be stated explicitly and evidenced.

Current architecture

Complete source repository, controlled public artifact.

The GitHub repository preserves content, release evidence, tests and tooling. The production Pages artifact publishes governed routes and their real dependencies. Repository size is therefore not treated as a public navigation structure or a deletion list.
