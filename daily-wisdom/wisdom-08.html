Daily Murugan Wisdom 08: Clean Speech

Words should heal, guide, or remain silent.

Back to Daily Wisdom

Reflection

Lord Murugan's Vel is not only a weapon; it is a symbol of clean decision, disciplined courage, and removal of inner confusion.

Use this teaching today through one concrete action. Do not make devotion abstract. Convert it into speech, work, patience, service, or restraint.

Practice

Chant Om Saravana Bhava 12 times slowly. Then complete one duty you have been postponing.

Do Not Miss

Spiritual progress is not measured by how much content you read. It is measured by how quickly your conduct becomes cleaner.
