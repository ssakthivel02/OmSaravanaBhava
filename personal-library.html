Privacy and interpretation boundary

  • The dashboard reads five existing browser-local datasets and writes no new browser data.
  • Counts are actual valid local records, not claims of complete Murugan literature or audio coverage.
  • Local records whose routes are invalid, duplicated, removed or not currently published are excluded.
  • Reading percentage is an approximate position, and local completion is not proof that a work was fully read.
  • This is not a recommendation engine, behavioural profile or analytics service.
  • No account, cloud synchronization or cross-device history is provided.
