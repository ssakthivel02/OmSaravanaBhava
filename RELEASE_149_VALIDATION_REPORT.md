## Publication controls

- The user-uploaded 2,191-page Thiruppugazh PDF is the primary repository source.
- Only manually checked full texts are published and indexed.
- OCR-only content is not emitted as production content.
- Gaps in numbering are deliberate and are not replaced with placeholder pages.
- No external audio, copied commentary or unverified translations are bundled.
