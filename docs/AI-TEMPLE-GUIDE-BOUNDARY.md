# AI Temple Guide Boundary

Current mode: local source-aware guide.

The interface:
- Loads only local temple JSON records.
- Does not call a third-party model.
- Reports unavailable data.
- Does not infer timings, contacts, distances, rituals or historical dates.
- Preserves the distinction between devotional tradition and verified history.

A future external AI integration must enforce citations, source allow-lists, safety controls and retrieval boundaries.
