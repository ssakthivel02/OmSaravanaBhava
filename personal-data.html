Data and safety boundary

  • Only five explicitly registered local datasets are supported: reading list, reading progress, reading notes, accessibility preferences and audio listening history.
  • Unknown datasets and invalid or duplicate records are ignored during validation.
  • Selected devotional text, published page bodies, offline cache contents, credentials and unrelated browser-storage keys are excluded.
  • Merge uses each dataset’s stable identifier and newest valid timestamp. Accessibility preferences are replaced as one bounded object.
  • No upload, cloud synchronization, analytics, server account or collaborative service is used.
  • A successful import is local operational evidence, not production certification.
