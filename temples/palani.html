Arupadai Veedu

பழனி முருகன் கோவில்

Palani Murugan Temple

Location: Palani, Dindigul

Wisdom, renunciation and Dhandayuthapani worship.

Open map

Publication status: Published with a primary-text citation. The literary citation supports the stated textual association; it does not claim a complete inscriptional or architectural history.

Literary witness

தாஇல் கொள்கை மடந்தையொடு சில்நாள் ஆவினன்குடி அசைதலும் உரியன்.

The third movement explicitly names Avinankudi and describes ascetics, musicians and celestial worshippers.

Citation: Thirumurugatruppadai, Part 3 — Thiru Avinankudi [Palani], lines 126–176.
