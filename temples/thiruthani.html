Arupadai Veedu

திருத்தணி முருகன் கோவில்

Thiruthani Murugan Temple

Location: Tiruttani

Hill shrine associated with peace, courage and inner calm.

Open map

Publication status: Published with a primary-text citation. The literary citation supports the stated textual association; it does not claim a complete inscriptional or architectural history.
Traditional association — explicitly labelled

Literary witness

செய்யன் சிவந்த ஆடையன் … குன்றுதொறு ஆடலும் நின்ற தன்பண்பே.

The fifth movement describes Murugan’s presence and dance across hill country. Release 147 labels the Tiruttani connection as a traditional association because the poem does not name Tiruttani directly in this section.

Citation: Thirumurugatruppadai, Part 5 — Kunruthoradal, lines 190–217.
