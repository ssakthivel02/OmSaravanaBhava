கந்தர் அனுபூதி — Introduction

கந்தர் அனுபூதி is a compact Murugan devotional and jnana work traditionally attributed to Arunagirinathar. This batch creates the structured reader layer for the website: verse pages, searchable JSON, SEO data, and validation notes.
