Reading and evidence boundary

  • No devotional text is copied into local progress storage.
  • Progress is an approximate scroll position, not proof that a work has been read or completed.
  • Each destination retains its existing publication, source-review, completeness and copyright statements.
  • Focus mode changes presentation only; it does not alter the source text.
  • Reading-list data, accessibility preferences and audio history remain separate and are not deleted by clearing progress.
  • No account, analytics or cross-device synchronization is used.
